Slurmtools provides scientists with an easy-to-use package for interacting with Slurm, offering the ability to submit (sbatch), monitor (squeue), and cancel (scancel) jobs. Job submissions are managed through a customizable template file, allowing users to tailor slurmtools to their unique needs.
